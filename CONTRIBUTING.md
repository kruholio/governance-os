## 1. Axiomatic Conformity

Every contribution (Pull Request) is validated against the core axioms of **governance-os**:

- **Mathematics is always right:** Logical fallacies or calculation errors result in immediate rejection.
    
- **Physics wins:** Proposals must operate within the constraints of thermodynamics and resource availability.
    
- **Love is love:** Measures must maintain absolute equality and minimize systemic bias.

## 2. Pull Request (PR) Workflow

To maintain scientific integrity, all PRs must follow this schema:

### L0: Data Validation (Desired, but probably not yet possible)

- Specify all metrics and data sources used.
    
- Measurement uncertainty must be $\epsilon < 3\%$. (Desired, but probably not yet possible)
    
- Sampling rates ($f_s$) must satisfy $f_s > 2 \cdot f_{max}$ to prevent aliasing in social feedback loops. (Desired, but probably not yet possible)
    

### L3: The "Albert Einstein Loss" Proof

Each PR must include a brief mathematical derivation showing that the change reduces the **Albert Einstein Loss** ($L_{AE}$):

$$L_{AE} = \sum_{i=1}^{N} \int (P_H \cdot \sigma_i(t)) \, dt \rightarrow \text{min}$$

Demonstrate that the efficiency coefficient ($\sigma$) of the affected "20-Watt units" increases through your proposal.

## 3. Anonymity, Identity & Choice

- **Optional Pseudonymity:** Contributors may participate using pseudonyms to keep the focus strictly on technical quality and logic.
    
- **Transparency of Choice:** Participation under legal names is equally permitted; the system remains neutral to the contributor's identity.
    
- **Signed Commits:** Regardless of identity choice, we recommend using GPG/SSH signatures to ensure the integrity and non-repudiation of your code.
    
- **Meritocratic Validation:** All Pull Requests are subjected to the same L1-Axiom checks, irrespective of the contributor's status or name.
    

This ensures that the "Hardware Specialists" and "Core Developers" can choose the level of public exposure that fits their personal risk model or professional standing.
